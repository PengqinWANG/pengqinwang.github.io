I am a Ph.D. candidate in HKUST Aerial Robotics Group, under the supervision of Prof. Shaojie Shen and Prof. Jun Ma. My research involves Robotics, Autonomous Driving, Foundation Model, Reinforcement Learning, and Decision-Making.

I received my B.Eng. degree from Shandong University, where I studied at Chongxin College(崇新学堂) and worked with Prof. Bin Zhou on mobile robots.
